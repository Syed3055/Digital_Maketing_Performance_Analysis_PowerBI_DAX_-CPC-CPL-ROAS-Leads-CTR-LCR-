## 📊 Google Ads Spend Performance Analysis – Power BI
### 🔍 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Google Ads spending data to evaluate marketing performance, conversion efficiency, and profitability using Power BI.
The primary objective is to track the full marketing funnel, identify cost efficiency, and understand how ad spend converts into revenue and profit.
The dashboard converts raw Google Ads data into actionable business insights for:

Marketing Managers
Business Owners
Performance Analysts


### 📄 Report Pages Overview
#### 1️⃣ Executive Summary
Purpose: High-level performance snapshot for decision-makers.
What this page shows:

Overall ad spend vs revenue
Profitability and efficiency KPIs
Campaign and ad group performance comparison
Top-performing search keywords

Key Insights from Executive Summary:

Total ad spend of 2.79K generated 12K payments
Strong profitability with 9.09K total profit
Healthy ROAS of 23.47%
Clear visibility into which campaigns and keywords drive impressions, clicks, and revenue


### 2️⃣ Marketing Funnel Overview
Purpose: Visual storytelling of the end-to-end conversion funnel.
Funnel Flow:
##### Ad Cost → Impressions → Clicks → Leads → Payments → Profit → ROAS
Why this page is important:

Shows where users drop off
Highlights cost effectiveness at each stage
Makes it easy to identify optimization opportunities

#### 📈 KPI Details with Actual Values
🔹 Total Cost – 2.79K
Total amount spent on Google Ads campaigns.
This represents the marketing investment.

🔹 Total Impressions – 7K
Number of times ads were shown to users.
Indicates campaign reach and visibility.

🔹 Total Clicks – 387
Number of users who clicked the ads.
Measures user interest and ad relevance.

🔹 Click Through Rate (CTR) – 5.56%
CTR = Clicks ÷ Impressions
Shows how effective ads are at attracting clicks.
✅ A CTR above 5% indicates good ad targeting and messaging.

🔹 Cost Per Click (CPC) – $7.21
CPC = Cost ÷ Clicks
Shows how much we pay per user interaction.
✅ Controlled CPC indicates efficient bidding strategy.

🔹 Total Leads – 12
Users who completed the desired action (lead generation).

🔹 Lead Conversion Rate (LCR) – 3.10%
LCR = Leads ÷ Clicks
Measures landing page and offer effectiveness.

🔹 Cost Per Lead (CPL) – $232.39
CPL = Cost ÷ Leads
Shows how much it costs to generate one lead.

🔹 Total Payments – 12K
Total revenue generated from ads.
Represents business income from marketing efforts.

🔹 Total Profit – 9.09K
Profit = Payments − Cost
Shows actual business gain after ad spend.

🔹 Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) – 23.47%
ROAS = (Profit ÷ Cost) × 100
Measures efficiency of ad spend.
✅ Strong ROAS indicates high profitability.

🔹 Profit Percentage – 76.53%
Indicates the percentage of revenue retained as profit.
✅ A high profit margin reflects excellent campaign efficiency.
